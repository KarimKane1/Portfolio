About Me

I'm a Senegalese-American engineer and former Division I athlete who loves learning, building, and solving problems. I recently graduated from Northeastern University's Honors College, where rowing and engineering shaped how I think about discipline, teamwork, and growth.

I'm driven by curiosity and enjoy figuring out how things can be done better, simpler, or more thoughtfully.
