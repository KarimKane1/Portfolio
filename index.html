About Me

I'm a Northeastern University honors student, Division 1 rowing athlete, and industrial engineering major. Over the course of my studies, I've had the opportunity to complete four internships in biotech, engineering materials, themed entertainment, and real estate. At Ginkgo Bioworks, Rogers Corporation, Walt Disney World, and Greystar through Project Destined, I gained hands-on experience in innovation, market research, operations, and financial analysis.

Outside of academics and work, I'm passionate about promoting diversity in rowing as a member of the Most Beautiful Thing Inclusion Fund. I'm also proud to have been named an IRCA Scholar-Athlete, which recognizes my dedication to both rowing and my studies.
